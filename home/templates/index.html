The main aim of the project is to design and come up with an effective yet self-reliant automated system that can be use by our faculty to randomly allocate seat for student and invigilator to an examination hall.

Student Login

Student can login and view their seat for an examination

Staff

Staff can login and view the hall they will be invigilating

Administrator

Manage system operations, halls and students

The Main Objective Of The Project

  1. *Investigate factors affecting the performances of students in examinations.
  2. *Analyse the existing system.
  3. *Design a new examination seat shuffling system.
  4. *Implement and develop the proposed system.
  5. *Provide secure access control of students and examination officers to the system.
  6. *Test and validate the new proposed system
  7. *To void redundancy
